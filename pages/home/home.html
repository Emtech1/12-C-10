Car Depreciation App

This car depreciation calculator is a handy tool that will help you estimate the value of your car once it's been used. You might be surprised, but the value of your car decreases to 91% of the initial market value the minute you purchase it.

The car value continues to drop year after year. After five years, your car's value decreases to 40% of the initial value.
